About Quincy Larson

Quincy Larson is the teacher who founded FreeCodeCamp, a charitable organization focused on providing free, world class CS education for all. I thank this man wholeheartedly for providing us with such a wonderful creation, which has allowed for me to have the skills to make websites such as this, and much more in the future.

Timeline

  • 2000 - Studied undergrad at US state school
  • 2001 - Becomes a school teacher
  • 2007 - Becomes a school director
  • 2013 - Transitions to software engineering
  • 2014 - Becomes 'the teacher who founded FreeCodeCamp.org'
